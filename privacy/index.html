Privacy Policy

This page explains how UKSOFT may collect, use, and protect information when you use its games and services.

1. General provisions

UKSOFT respects user privacy. By using UKSOFT games or other services, you agree to the principles described on this page.

2. Information that may be collected

Depending on the specific app, technical data necessary for proper operation, analytics, and service improvement may be collected, including device type, operating system, language settings, IP address, advertising identifiers, crash data, and in-app interaction data.

UKSOFT does not request unnecessary personal data without a valid reason. The scope of data may depend on the functionality of a particular game or integrated third-party services.

3. How information is used

Collected information may be used to support app functionality, improve stability, analyze errors, display advertising, measure usage analytics, and improve the quality of UKSOFT products.

4. Third-party services

Some apps may use third-party services that have their own data handling policies. This may include, for example, Google Play Services, AdMob, Unity, and Firebase.

You are also encouraged to review the privacy policies of the relevant third-party service providers.

5. Advertising and cookies

Some games may contain advertising or technologies similar to cookies that are used by third-party libraries for personalization, analytics, and service improvement.

6. Data security

UKSOFT strives to use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage can guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's privacy

UKSOFT services are not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided such information, please contact UKSOFT to request its removal.

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version is published on this page and becomes effective upon posting.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact: uksooft@gmail.com
